Welcome to Pankh

For the event, we would be making a multi page website for our fictional organisation ‘PANKH : Wings to All’. We decided upon the name based on our vision to provide wings that is pankh to the less privileged specially in these hard times of the pandemic. PANKH works towards 3 goals being to provide employment, healthcare as well us online education in various domains to the needy. Our website aims to enable PANKH to reach out to more people as we look forward to a better world for all of us.

Our Causes

Under ongoing crisis of COVID-19 our Pankh society focuses on neutralizing following negative impacts of the covid virus. The areas that our organization work on are

SKILL TRAINING

Pankh is working towards providing employment to people. Our social venture would be working towards providing free vocational courses and skill training to unemployed youth as well as women who come from marginalised communities. It will provide them with sustainable livelihood.

Know More

HEALTHCARE

PANKH is currently engaged in provision of masks, soaps and ration kits to underprivileged families. Our efforts are also focused on creating awareness regarding hygiene, sanitation and natural methods of increasing one’s immunity via ayurvedic herbs herbs that are easily available.

Know More

EDUCATION

PANKH has been providing free education facilities in various domains like in English, life skills, arithmetic, languages and computer education. Covid 19 required us to shift from classroom teaching to online platforms. Pankh is constanly trying new methods for online education.
