Milestones

2005

The foundation of PANKH was laid by 2 friends passionate to work for a cause Dr. Vishambh Das and Dr. Mrigul Raj. The story of PANKH began from the basement of an old delhi house, as an healthcare orientated organisation.

2010

5 years of growth and working for the underprivileged, PANKH family grew up to 900 volunteers across 37 cities, with 583 free checkups provided and 1000+ ration kits distributed.

2015

Expanding our drives to more noble causes like skill training and quality education, PANKH had 1063 craftspeople registered and 260+ families were given a sustainable livelihood, and 4800+ underprivileged students being taught in 62 centres across the country

2020

26,000+ and 100+ cities later, PANKH is proud to say that we have provided 10,000 free healthcare checkups, 10M + ration kits distributed, 700+ families given a sustainable livelihood and 19,000 + students registered under our education drive.
