Healthcare for all

PANKH aims to bridge the gap in the availability of low-cost primary health care for rural and urban underprivileged communities.

Healthcare

PANKH strives to provide low-cost primary healthcare services to underprivileged communities by setting up a chain of primary healthcare clinics which provide medical consultation and medicines at very low subsidised prices.

We also provide subsidized eye and dental check ups along with Cataract surgeries, Root canals and cavity fillings in collaboration with Dr. Menson’s Eye and Dental Hospitals.

Covid 19 Project -

PANKH is currently engaged in provision of masks, soaps and ration kits to underprivileged families.

Our efforts are also focused on creating awareness regarding hygiene, sanitation and natural methods of increasing one’s immunity via Heart-leaved moonseed (Giloy), Neem and other similar ayurvedic herbs that are easily available.

All health care centres are sanitized multiple times and follow all precautions related to Covid 19.
