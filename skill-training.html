Teaching skills to unskilled

At PANKH we provide free vocational courses and skill training to unemployed youth and women who come from marginalised communities.
Skill training courses -
1. Tailoring
2. Basic IT courses
3. Henna Designing and Application
4. Conversational English

Tailoring

PANKH also provides courses in basic handicraft and embroidery to women to make them self-sufficient, reduce inequalities and provide a secondary means of earning.

Embroidery courses -
1. Zardosi
2. Kaudi Stitch
3. Toda embroidery

Our enrolled craftspeople also tailor masks used by them and their family. They are also bought by the organisation at market prices and further provided to the needy under our healthcare mission for free.

Some masks are also embroidered by our talented artists. These are sold and the profit is used to take our mission of skill training to new heights.

All health care centres are sanitized multiple times and follow all precautions related to Covid 19.
